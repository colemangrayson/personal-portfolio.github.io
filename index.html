About Me

          I am an aspiring physician-scientist driven by a passion to provide total medicine to the patients of tomorrow. “Total medicine” is an active approach to medicine; it is a model where physicians not only provide direct patient care, but also advance scientific research, advocate for effective public health policy, and engage with communities to break down systemic barriers to healthcare. It goes beyond the clinic and bridges the gaps between research, the private sector, government, and more.

          With hands-on experience in everything from pediatric healthcare to computer science, I've explored the intersections of numerous industries with medicine. From investigating autoimmune mechanisms in the lab to developing AI tools for clinical trials and from advocating for health access as a health insurance navigator to writing science articles that inform and inspire, my work is rooted in service, curiosity, and impact. I aim to pursue a career where I can care deeply, think rigorously, and innovate boldly to advance medicine that is both cutting-edge and compassionate.
