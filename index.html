About Me

Bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and compassionate healthcare

I am an aspiring physician-scientist driven by a passion for bridging clinical care with translational research and digital innovation. With hands-on experience in pediatric healthcare, immunology research, and self-taught computer science, I've explored the intersections of patient care, molecular science, and health technology. From investigating autoimmune mechanisms in the lab to developing AI tools for clinical trials, and from advocating for health access as a health insurance navigator to writing science articles that inform and inspire, my work is rooted in service, curiosity, and impact. I aim to pursue a career where I can care deeply, think rigorously, and innovate boldly to advance medicine that is both cutting-edge and compassionate.
